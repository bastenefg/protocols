Pipette Calibration

Frequency: Once per year Vendor: VWR / Avantor

Note

MIT also hosts an onsite Pipette Calibration Clinic twice per year. This is often more convenient than shipping, since pipettes are only gone for one day.

Overview

Pipette calibration ensures accurate and reproducible liquid handling for all experiments. This protocol describes how to schedule calibration through VWR/Avantor, prepare pipettes, and either ship them for service or use the onsite clinic at MIT.

Annual Calibration Options

  1. Ship pipettes to VWR/Avantor using their calibration service.

  2. Attend the MIT onsite pipette calibration clinic (twice per year).

Shipping Procedure

  1. Contact the current VWR/Avantor account manager for MIT. Request a calibration quote for the pipettes requiring service.

  2. Forward the quote to the lab administrator for approval and purchase. The “Standard” calibration plan is typically sufficient.

  3. Prepare pipettes for shipping:

    • Ensure all pipettes are clean and free of contamination.

    • Disassemble if needed, following manufacturer guidelines. Instructions can be found online, for example here:

      https://www.pipettesupplies.com/blog-vwr/preventive-maintenance-vwr-ergonomic-high-performance-ehp-single-channel-pipette/

    • Allow all parts to dry completely before packing.

  4. Ship the pipettes:

    • Use a shipping box from previous years if available.

    • If none are available, VWR will provide a shipping box when requested.
